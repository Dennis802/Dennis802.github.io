- Horrifying faces -

Band

Ecocide was formed in Haarlem, the Netherlands back in 2012 by Sten, Bas and Rick, with Dennis joining in 2014. The first self-named EP set the standard for recurring subjects, with themes of H.P. Lovecraft, 80's horror and sci-fi. Their 2013 album "Eye of Wicked Sight" shifted their Thrash Metal sound into Death Metal and attracted a bigger, international following.

They shared the stage with Malevolent Creation, Sinister, Cannabis Corpse and the legendary Bolt Thrower. After two line-up changes the band went into hiatus in 2015, returning in 2017. After many years their long awaited second full length album will be released in 2023.
