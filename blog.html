METAMORPHOSIS coming this year!

It has been 10 years since the release of our first album, Eye of Wicked Sight. To celebrate this occasion, we will be releasing the long-awaited second full-length album on July 22, 2023. Filled to the brim with old school death metal from start to finish. Those who love Death, Sepultura, Gorguts, and Pestilence, this one is for you! And if a full-length album doesn't fulfill your needs, several live recordings of our 2015 concert in Willem Twee, Den Bosch, will be released on YouTube. Here's to 10 years of old school death metal, and many more to follow!
